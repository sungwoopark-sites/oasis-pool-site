Our Services

Comprehensive pool care for commercial properties throughout Orange County

💧

Pool Service & Maintenance

Routine upkeep including vacuuming, sweeping, tile cleaning, algae removal, surface skimming, strainer box cleaning, chemical testing and pH balancing, water level refilling, filter backwashing, equipment checks, and safety gate inspections.

🏢

Commercial Pool Services

Comprehensive commercial pool care for HOAs, apartment/condo complexes, commercial properties, and community pools. We take the hassle out of maintenance, compliance, and repairs.

🔧

Repairs & Remodels

Pool renovations, filtration system replacements, pump and filter upgrades, and projects of any size. We restore pools to their best condition.

📅

Scheduled Maintenance

Quarterly and bi-monthly services including filter disassembly and acid washing, tank cleaning, diatomaceous earth replacement, chlorinator servicing, total dissolved solids testing, and safety equipment inspections.

About Oasis Pool Service

Oasis Pool Services is a family owned & operated commercial pool service provider in Southern California. We emphasize continuous improvement, professionalism, and dependability.

We serve a diverse clientele including HOAs, apartment buildings, mobile home parks, hotels, and communities throughout Orange County. Our team has expertise in water chemistry standards, LSI (Langelier Saturation Index), flow rates, TDH (total dynamic head), and pool technology.
